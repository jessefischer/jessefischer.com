Jesse Fischer
pianist.

Jesse Fischer is a Brooklyn-based pianist and composer, known for combining elements of his own Jewish heritage with music of the African diaspora, jazz, hip-hop, and singer-songwriter genres. read more

producer.

Jesse also is an in-depend producer and songwriter in the indie-pop, soul, and singer-songwriter genres, working with artists like Morgan James, Laura Izibor, Elise Testone, and Brenda Nicole Moorer. read more

engineer.

As a recording and mix engineer, Jesse has a unique sound that’s equal parts Abbey Road, Stax Records, and Tribe Called Quest. His credits include work with many cutting edge artists including Christian Scott, BIGYUKI, and Justin Brown. read more
